Profile

Rae Palmer Hairdressing is a haven for A-list hair and beauty services on the South Coast, and has been nominated as one of the leading independent salons in the UK (HAIR Magazine's 2006/2007/2008 Consumer Awards).

  • Rae Palmer

Rae is the Creative Director for Rae Palmer Hairdressing, an ambassador for Schwarzkopf Professional.

Rae holds the prestigious L'Oreal Colour Specialist Degree, was nominated as Best Salon Manager in the British Hairdressing Business Awards 2006 / 2007, won hairdressings 'Dragon's Den' style competition Dream Factory and has received a Southern hairdresser of the year award in the prestigious British hairdressing awards 2008.
